## FADE IN:

### 1. EXT. EVELIE — KNOLL HOMESTEAD — RED EVENING

A red sun hangs over fields of pale grain.

The light is flat and tired. It catches on a small farmhouse and a paper kite stranded in a tree.

Warm windows. Supper sounds.

Ordinary life, still happening.

### 2. INT. KNOLL HOMESTEAD — KITCHEN — CONTINUOUS

Four people at a scarred wooden table.

ANDY KNOLL, broad hands, dirt still beneath his nails. MARYE KNOLL, quick-eyed, tired in the comfortable way. TOMME, five, waiting with absolute concentration.

RUTHYE MARYE KNOLL, thirteen, repairs Tomme's paper kite instead of eating. Her braids are pulled tight. So is the rest of her.

TOMME  
Will it fly?

RUTHYE  
I said it would.

She threads a new spar through the paper. Precise work.

MARYE  
Ruthye. Eat.

RUTHYE  
The split will spread.

ANDY  
The kite has survived worse than five minutes.

TOMME  
She promised.

Ruthye looks at him. That settles it.

Marye moves Ruthye's bowl closer to the kite.

MARYE  
Then finish the knot.

Andy tears bread and drops a piece onto Ruthye's plate. Tomme leans so close to her work that his hair catches in the tail.

Ruthye frees him without looking up.

The family eats around her seriousness. They know how.

A KNOCK at the front door.

Not a crash. Not a raid.

A polite knock.

Andy stills. Marye's hand settles on Tomme's shoulder.

The second knock is just as patient.

Andy opens the door.

KREM stands on the porch with three BRIGANDS behind him. Dusty. Armed. Tired from travel. A black metal crossbow rides across Krem's back.

KREM  
Evening.

Andy looks past them. Distant lights sit in his field where no lights should be.

ANDY  
Can I help you?

Krem steps inside.

KREM  
You can try.

The Brigands follow. One remains at the door. Two move through the kitchen into the rest of the house.

Krem sits in Andy's chair. He notices the pie cooling on the sill.

MARYE  
That is for tomorrow.

KREM  
I have had a long day.

He cuts a slice with the family knife.

Andy stays standing.

ANDY  
What do you need?

KREM  
Food. Fuel. Tools. Anything that seals a hull or opens one.

ANDY  
Take what you need and go.

Krem tastes the pie. Nods once to Marye. Genuine approval.

The Brigands begin filling sacks.

Ruthye watches their hands. Counts the weapons. Counts the door.

Krem notices.

KREM  
Bilki had yellow hills.

No one asked. He tells it anyway.

KREM (CONT'D)  
You could see them before you saw the cities. When the sky started going gray, we sent for help.

A Brigand pulls preserved food from a cabinet.

KREM (CONT'D)  
The trade lanes moved around us. The ports closed. Everyone had a reason. Their own wars. Their own harvests. Their own children.

ANDY  
I'm sorry.

KREM  
So were they.

Krem holds Andy's gaze for a beat. Then he returns to the pie.

The youngest Brigand pulls down a small cloth sack marked for the next planting. Andy moves before he thinks.

ANDY  
Leave that.

The room changes.

Krem looks at Andy. Then, without heat, he strikes Marye across the face.

Andy lunges.

The Brigands catch him and drive him across the table. Bowls break. Tomme screams.

Ruthye pulls Tomme behind her.

Andy fights until Krem raises one hand.

KREM  
Enough.

The Brigands stop.

Andy is on his knees. Marye holds a cloth to her mouth. Ruthye stands between Tomme and the room.

Krem studies Andy.

KREM (CONT'D)  
You protected your wife.

He looks toward Marye.

KREM (CONT'D)  
I would have thought less of you if you hadn't.

He gestures.

The Brigands release Andy.

The raiders' sacks are full. The planting grain lies where it was dropped. Krem wipes the family knife and puts it down.

It is over.

Everyone in the room feels it become over.

Krem walks to the door.

The road waits outside. His men carry everything he came for.

He passes Ruthye.

She looks up at him.

No tears. No lowered eyes. Tomme shaking behind her, one of Ruthye's hands holding him there. The other hangs empty at her side.

Krem recognizes the judgment in her face.

For a moment, something almost human moves through his.

Then he chooses.

He takes the crossbow from his back.

Marye understands first.

MARYE  
No.

Krem seats a plain iron bolt. Andy steps in front of his family.

Krem aims at Andy.

His eyes remain on Ruthye.

KREM  
This is what the world is.

He fires.

The bolt drives Andy back against the table.

Ruthye turns and covers Tomme's eyes before he sees their father fall.

Krem leaves.

The Brigands follow. Their footsteps recede across the porch and down the road.

Marye drops beside Andy.

Ruthye holds Tomme against her. The repaired kite lies beneath the broken dishes.

Her eyes stay on the open door.

## CUT TO BLACK.

### 3. EXT. KNOLL HOMESTEAD — CAIRN — RED MORNING

Days later.

Fresh stones rise beside the road.

Marye stands with Tomme beneath the red sun. The bruise on her face has yellowed at the edges.

Ruthye wears a travel coat. A narrow sword at her hip. A satchel across her body.

She kneels in front of Tomme and gives him the kite.

The torn wing has been repaired. Not beautifully. Completely.

TOMME  
You fixed it.

RUTHYE  
I said I would.

He grips the frame with both hands.

Marye looks at the sword. Then at her daughter.

MARYE  
I can't stop you.

Ruthye says nothing.

Marye steps close and holds her. Ruthye is rigid for one breath, then not.

MARYE (CONT'D)  
Come home.

RUTHYE  
I will.

Marye lets her go because holding harder will not keep her.

Ruthye walks down the red road toward the port.

Tomme lifts the kite. It catches the weak wind and rises.

### 4. INT. KARA'S SHIP — DREAM

Black.

A WHITE CITY drifts beneath a broken sky.

A planet burns behind it.

Green light beneath a woman's skin.

Hands folding a funeral cloth.

A puppy crying somewhere in the dark.

A father's palm against curved glass.

A pod tearing free.

ARGO shrinking in the window until the stars swallow it.

A soundless FLASH—

### 5. INT. KARA'S SHIP — SLEEPING BERTH — RED MORNING

KARA ZOR-EL wakes with her hand already reaching.

Her fingers close around a bottle on the floor.

A white paw pins it down.

Kara follows the paw to KRYPTO. Large, bright-eyed, entirely awake.

KARA  
Traitor.

Krypto's paw presses harder.

Kara slides the bottle sideways. He lets her have it because this was never going to be a fair contest.

She drinks before her feet touch the floor.

The berth is lived-in past the point of excuse. Clothes. Tools. Empty cups. A faded blue suit beneath an oversized leather jacket.

The red sun fills the porthole.

SHIP VOICE  
Good morning. Primary drive inspection remains overdue.

KARA  
Still morning?

SHIP VOICE  
Locally.

Kara finds yesterday's trousers.

SHIP VOICE (CONT'D)  
One family message is marked urgent.

Kara stops.

KARA  
Play it.

CLARK'S VOICE fills the berth. Warm, careful, trying not to sound careful.

CLARK (V.O.)  
Hey. Happy birthday. Twenty-one.

Kara closes her eyes.

CLARK (V.O.) (CONT'D)  
Ma made a cake. She says that isn't pressure. It is pressure. Call me when you wake up.

A beat.

CLARK (V.O.) (CONT'D)  
Or when you feel like waking up.

The message ends.

SHIP VOICE  
Return call?

Kara pulls on the faded suit.

KARA  
No.

She fills Krypto's bowl, checks the old scar behind one ear, and cleans something from the corner of his eye with her thumb.

Krypto leans his entire weight into her.

KARA  
You have terrible boundaries.

He licks her face.

For him, she smiles.

She takes the leather jacket and the bottle.

The maintenance warning follows her to the hatch.

SHIP VOICE  
Drive inspection—

The hatch closes.

### 6. INT. EVELIE PORT BAR — RED DAY

The port bar is dark enough to forget the sun outside.

Kara occupies a back booth. Krypto sprawls beneath the table, chin across her boots. The BARTENDER has been adding water to Kara's glass without discussing it.

The room is full of freight hands, pilots, farmers waiting on parts, and people avoiding one more departure.

Ruthye enters.

Dust on her coat. Sword at her hip. She scans the room once.

A PORT RUFFIAN catches her arm as she passes.

RUFFIAN  
They let children carry steel now?

Ruthye turns through his grip. His wrist folds. His knife leaves his belt and appears in her hand.

She drives it into the bar top, handle first toward him.

RUTHYE  
They let you.

She continues to the counter.

The ruffian studies the knife. Decides he has somewhere else to be.

RUTHYE  
I need the strongest person in this port who can be hired.

The Bartender's eyes move to Kara's booth.

Kara raises her watered glass in a small refusal.

Ruthye walks over and stands across from her.

RUTHYE  
Are you the strongest person here?

KARA  
Depends what time it is.

RUTHYE  
Now.

KARA  
Then no.

Ruthye sits anyway.

Krypto opens one eye.

RUTHYE  
I require help pursuing a man named Krem.

The name does not reach Kara. The next sentence does.

RUTHYE (CONT'D)  
He murdered my father.

Kara lowers the glass.

RUTHYE (CONT'D)  
Blood taken outside the heat of battle is answered by the nearest capable kin. I am eldest. The debt is mine.

KARA  
That's bloodthirsty for Evelie.

Ruthye's jaw tightens.

RUTHYE  
It is the Code.

KARA  
How old are you?

RUTHYE  
Thirteen.

KARA  
You're thirteen.

RUTHYE  
Yes.

Kara studies her. The coat. The sword. The exhaustion held upright by certainty.

KARA  
Your mother alive?

Ruthye hesitates.

RUTHYE  
Yes.

KARA  
Brothers? Sisters?

RUTHYE  
A brother.

KARA  
Go home.

RUTHYE  
I will.

KARA  
Now, kid.

RUTHYE  
I promised when the debt was paid.

Something in Kara's face gives way, then closes.

KARA  
Go home before your mother has to bury you too.

RUTHYE  
You believe I am chasing a ship already gone.

She lifts a grungy metal-and-glass case from her satchel and puts it on the table.

Inside, an amber JUMP-SEED turns slowly in its cradle.

Kara's attention sharpens through the drink.

RUTHYE (CONT'D)  
They are still on Evelie because of this.

KARA  
You stole their drive seed.

RUTHYE  
While they were selling what they took. Their ship can reach orbit. It cannot leave the system.

KARA  
And you brought it into a crowded bar.

RUTHYE  
I brought it to the strongest person in the port.

KARA  
We covered that.

Ruthye takes a palm-sized reader from her satchel and holds its probe against the case.

The seed wakes.

CHIRP.

Kara goes still.

CHIRP. CHIRP.

KARA  
Close it.

RUTHYE  
Why?

KARA  
That isn't a status tone. It's calling home.

Ruthye snaps the case shut.

The reader remains lit in her other hand.

The bar door opens.

Krem stands there with three Brigands.

The black crossbow rides in his hand.

He sees Ruthye.

Then the case beneath her palm.

KREM  
There you are.

Krypto rises beneath the table.

### 7. INT. EVELIE PORT BAR — CONTINUOUS

Krem walks toward the booth. His people spread through the room.

KREM  
Return it.

Ruthye stands.

RUTHYE  
Return what you took.

Krem looks at her sword. Almost weary.

KREM  
You should have stayed home.

Kara slides out of the booth between them.

KARA  
Everybody gets one chance to leave without making my day worse.

One Brigand swings the butt of a rifle at her.

The room detonates.

Kara moves in three truths.

A BURST—she crosses the space too fast, catches the rifle before it lands, and redirects it into a table.

A TOO-HARD HIT—her palm touches the Brigand's chest. He goes through two chairs and does not rise.

A STUMBLE—the red sun and the bottle arrive together. Kara catches herself against a post.

She stares at her own hand. So does Krem.

Ruthye goes low. Her sword cuts a caster strap, not flesh. She drives her shoulder into a Brigand's knee.

Krypto launches from beneath the booth and clamps onto an armored forearm.

Krem does not brawl. He watches Kara fail to become what she almost is.

Then he raises the crossbow.

The bolt in it is black-tipped and wet.

He aims at Kara.

RUTHYE  
Kara—

Krypto lets go and leaps.

The bolt hits him beneath the shoulder.

He lands hard.

For Kara, the room stops.

Black threads spread under white fur.

KARA  
No.

A Brigand reaches the booth and takes the jump-seed case.

Ruthye sees it. Starts toward him.

Kara is already on the floor beside Krypto. Krypto's head rolls in her lap.

Ruthye stops.

She turns and clears a path to the door instead, cutting a fallen table loose from Kara's route.

Krem receives the seed. Checks the pulse through its casing.

At his throat, beneath torn armor, a dark glass vial catches the light.

He looks down at Ruthye.

KREM  
Go home.

Kara lifts Krypto. The effort nearly folds her.

Ruthye gets beneath his other side.

Krem and the Brigands retreat through the wrecked bar.

Kara does not follow.

### 8. EXT. EVELIE PORT — CONTINUOUS

Kara runs with Krypto in her arms.

Not super-speed. A desperate, uneven sprint.

Ruthye runs ahead, shoving carts clear.

Behind them, a Brigand transport rises from the launch field. Its engines change pitch as the recovered seed comes online.

Ruthye's reader wakes in her hand.

An amber line points after it.

She looks at the departing ship.

Then at Krypto.

She follows Kara.

### 9. INT. KARA'S SHIP — MEDBAY — MOMENTS LATER

Krypto lies on a diagnostic cradle. Kara's hands move across the controls too quickly and not quickly enough.

Ruthye seals the hatch behind them.

SHIP VOICE  
Foreign projectile. Circulatory contamination. Progressive neurotoxin.

KARA  
Counteragent.

SHIP VOICE  
Compound architecture incomplete. No synthesis template available.

KARA  
Build one.

SHIP VOICE  
This facility is not equipped to reverse-engineer a proprietary compound during active systemic collapse.

KARA  
Stabilize him.

A clear field closes around Krypto. His breathing eases by one terrible degree.

On the display, a broad amber band begins contracting toward red. No numbers.

RUTHYE  
Will he live?

KARA  
Yes.

Too fast.

The ship does not agree or disagree.

Kara calls up a star chart.

KARA  
Nearest yellow star.

One point brightens beyond the system.

The medbay runs a model: Krypto beneath yellow light. The poison remains. His failing systems strengthen around it.

RUTHYE  
It does not cure him.

KARA  
It might keep him alive long enough to find what does.

Ruthye looks at the red sun through the hatch window.

RUTHYE  
If yellow light makes you strong, why do you live under a red sun?

Kara checks the stabilizing field. Does not look at her.

KARA  
That's the point.

The ship interrupts before the silence can ask for more.

SHIP VOICE  
Course plotted. Primary drive inspection overdue. Maximum-output departure not advised.

Kara moves toward the cockpit.

Ruthye holds up the reader. The amber bearing points into the sky.

RUTHYE  
Krem is moving.

KARA  
Sun first.

RUTHYE  
He has the poison.

KARA  
And maybe the cure. Krypto doesn't have time for maybe.

She points to a manual pressure valve beside the cradle.

KARA (CONT'D)  
When that needle reaches red, pull down and hold it.

RUTHYE  
Until when?

Kara looks at Krypto.

KARA  
Use judgment.

### 10. INT./EXT. KARA'S SHIP — LAUNCH / DEEP SPACE — CONTINUOUS

The ship tears free of Evelie.

Red atmosphere falls away. Stars open.

Ruthye stares through the glass at her first open sky.

Then Krypto cries out.

She tears her eyes away.

Kara straps into the cockpit.

KARA  
Emergency output. Everything else into medbay.

SHIP VOICE  
Life support is included in everything else.

KARA  
Keep enough for the kid.

RUTHYE  
And you.

KARA  
Sure.

The drive cycles. Roughly.

SHIP VOICE  
Jump-coil temperature outside nominal range.

KARA  
Open the path.

The stars stretch.

The ship JUMPS.

Light swallows it.

It slams back into ordinary space. Warning glyphs flower across the cockpit.

They have moved.

Not far enough.

The route model redraws. The yellow star remains beyond the contracting patient band.

SHIP VOICE  
Yellow-star arrival now exceeds modeled patient viability.

Kara stares at the two lines that never meet.

Then reaches for the override.

SHIP VOICE (CONT'D)  
Jump coil requires cooling and inspection.

KARA  
Again.

Ruthye looks from Krypto to Kara.

RUTHYE  
You will break the ship.

KARA  
Ships can be fixed.

RUTHYE  
Can dogs?

Kara forces the control down.

The drive HOWLS.

In the medbay, the pressure needle snaps into red.

Ruthye throws herself onto the valve. It bucks beneath her hands.

RUTHYE  
It will not stay down.

KARA  
Strap it to the lower ring.

RUTHYE  
What strap?

KARA  
Any strap.

Ruthye rips a restraint from the wall, loops it through the valve, and leans back with all her weight.

The stabilizing field steadies.

The jump field forms crookedly around the ship. Light leaks through panel seams.

SHIP VOICE  
Abort advised.

Kara looks at Krypto on the monitor.

KARA  
No.

She commits the jump.

Metal SCREAMS.

For an instant the ship moves everywhere at once.

Then the ship's pale-blue jump coil burns white.

The universe throws them back.

The ship tumbles into darkness. Kara fights the controls with both hands, catches the spin, loses it, catches it again.

Every display dies.

Blackness.

Kara's breath.

Ruthye's.

Krypto's assisted rasp.

Emergency systems return one at a time.

SHIP VOICE  
Jump capacity lost. Conventional propulsion degraded. Automatic safe-harbor protocol engaged.

One location pulses nearby: a deep-space service and transit station wrapped around an old refueling spine.

Kara tries the drive control again.

Nothing.

SHIP VOICE (CONT'D)  
Nearest yellow star is no longer reachable.

Kara looks back at Krypto.

The ship turns toward the station.

Ahead, fluorescent light grows in the black.

## END ACT I

## ACT II

### 11. EXT. OASIS STATION — DEEP SPACE

Fluorescent light grows into a town.

OASIS STATION has accreted around an ancient refueling spine: docks, clinics, repair bays, market tiers, and transit tubes added whenever somebody needed one more reason not to die between stars.

Traffic swarms around it. Signs burn in dozens of languages.

OASIS STATION  
FUEL • FOOD • MEDICINE  
LAST ONE FOR A WHILE

After Evelie's exhausted red and the dark inside Kara's ship, the color is almost violent.

A station tug catches the damaged ship and draws it into a crowded berth.

### 12. INT. OASIS STATION — DOCK CLINIC — LATER

Krypto floats in an amber suspension tube. The black branches beneath his fur have stopped spreading. They have not receded.

An old public-service ROBODOC glides around the tube on a ceiling rail. Its white enamel has yellowed at the joints. Repairs from several decades do not quite match. A smiling star sticker clings to its chest.

It listens to Krypto's heart for a long time.

ROBODOC  
Strong heart.

KARA  
Best dog in the universe.

ROBODOC  
Not a medical category. But noted in patient file.

A small printer inside its chest CHATTERS. The Robodoc adds the strip to Krypto's chart.

Ruthye stands at the foot of the tube, her reader still pulsing faintly in her hand.

KARA  
Can you cure him?

ROBODOC  
No.

The answer is quiet, not cold.

ROBODOC (CONT'D)  
Your ship's assessment was correct. The counteragent is paired during manufacture. This clinic cannot reproduce it from the toxin alone.

RUTHYE  
Who has it?

ROBODOC  
Ranking Brigands carry neutralizer. Their weapons are not selective about whom they poison.

Ruthye remembers the dark vial at Krem's throat.

RUTHYE  
Krem has it.

Kara looks at her.

RUTHYE (CONT'D)  
I saw it.

KARA  
Then we know where the cure is.

ROBODOC  
You do not yet know where Krem is.

Ruthye lifts the reader.

RUTHYE  
I will.

The Robodoc brings up Kara's yellow-star model. Krypto's body strengthens beneath the projected light; the black compound remains bound through his blood.

ROBODOC  
Genuine yellow-star exposure might have increased his resistance. It would not have removed the poison.

Kara looks from the model to the black compound in Krypto's blood.

KARA  
How long can you hold him?

The Robodoc studies the broad patient band.

ROBODOC  
Hours with confidence. Perhaps a day without it.

KARA  
Make it the day.

ROBODOC  
I will make it every hour I can.

Kara puts her palm to the tube.

Krypto's paw drifts in the amber fluid. Slowly, it settles against the other side of the glass.

KARA  
I'm coming back.

His paw stays with hers.

KARA (CONT'D)  
You keep breathing until I do.

Ruthye lowers her reader.

### 13. INT. OASIS STATION — REPAIR BAY — LATER

VESK, a broad, four-armed mechanic with a queue of ships and no patience left for any of them, reaches into Kara's open drive housing.

Two arms strip out the blackened jump coil. A third keeps a diagnostic lead from falling loose. The fourth is already preparing a growth vat.

VESK  
Two weeks.

KARA  
Are you kidding me?

VESK  
I moved you ahead of three freighters and a wedding ship. Does that sound playful?

He drops the ruined coil into a metal tray. It breaks apart on contact.

KARA  
I need it in hours.

VESK  
You needed an inspection six months ago.

Kara glances at him.

VESK (CONT'D)  
The ship keeps records. Ships complain to mechanics because pilots don't listen.

KARA  
Can you adapt something from stock?

VESK  
Not to that housing. I can restore local thrust. A jump coil has to be grown.

KARA  
Then start growing it.

Vesk points with his chin.

Behind him, nutrient fluid is already flooding the vat.

VESK  
You think I took it apart for the conversation?

Kara reaches for her money.

VESK (CONT'D)  
Pay me when you have a ship worth charging for. Go.

He rolls beneath the drive before she can answer.

VESK (O.S.) (CONT'D)  
I have two weeks of work to do.

She leaves him to it.

### 14. INT. OASIS STATION — INFORMATION AND TICKETS — LATER

One ATTENDANT staffs a desk meant for four people. A meal has gone cold beside his terminal. He wears the expression of someone whose shift ended emotionally several hours ago.

Above him, a public starmap turns through shipping lanes and inhabited systems.

Ruthye places her reader beneath it. The device contains the intermittent bearings it caught each time Krem's recovered seed entered jump.

She rotates the record against the public routes. One amber trace crosses another. A quarantined world lights where they converge.

BILKI.

Ruthye knows the name.

ATTENDANT  
You don't want that one.

KARA  
What is it?

ATTENDANT  
Dead. Quarantined. It was the Brigand homeworld.

He opens the advisory without enthusiasm.

ATTENDANT (CONT'D)  
Collapsed biosphere. Toxic atmosphere. Radiation weather. Automated defenses with nobody left to shut them down.

RUTHYE  
Krem spoke of it.

KARA  
Looks like he went back.

Ruthye points to a thin public route brushing Bilki's orbit.

RUTHYE  
Does this line stop there?

The Attendant checks. He wishes he had not.

ATTENDANT  
The intergalactic coach? Technically.

Kara looks at Ruthye.

RUTHYE  
What do we do now?

KARA  
(deadpan)  
Take the bus.

She looks back to the Attendant.

KARA (CONT'D)  
How much?

He names a figure.

Kara empties several currencies from her jacket. The Attendant sorts them without comment.

His eyes settle on Ruthye.

ATTENDANT (CONT'D)  
Passenger under local majority. Are you her guardian?

Kara looks at Ruthye.

After a beat —

KARA  
Yes.

The Attendant takes in the torn jacket, the bruises, the red eyes. Then Ruthye's sword.

He decides the shift has asked enough of him.

Two tickets print.

ATTENDANT  
Last coach. Gate six. It departs in two hours.

He takes the fare and slides the tickets across.

ATTENDANT (CONT'D)  
Atmosphere protection is not included.

He turns to the next blinking light on his console.

### 15. INT. OASIS STATION — MARKET / FOOD COURT — LATER

With two hours until departure, Kara and Ruthye move through the station market.

Life has crowded into every usable meter.

Pilots sleep beneath departure boards. Children chase a suitcase that has grown legs. Vendors offer clothes, tools, and travel supplies from a hundred worlds.

Ruthye tries not to look like she is looking.

Kara lets her.

At a safety stall, Kara buys two compact atmosphere rings rated for Bilki. Ruthye turns hers over in her hand before slipping it onto her finger.

They wander on. Steam rolls from food stalls in colors Ruthye has never seen in food.

At the edge of the food court, Ruthye's attention catches for half a second on a pastry case.

LATER —

Kara drops into a molded plastic chair and sets down a tray. Two bruised cups of broth. Two of the saddest little cupcakes in the galaxy.

RUTHYE  
What are those?

KARA  
Happy birthday.

RUTHYE  
It is not my birthday.

KARA  
No. It's mine.

Ruthye looks at the cakes. Then at Kara.

RUTHYE  
Today?

KARA  
Apparently.

RUTHYE  
How old are you?

KARA  
Twenty-one.

Kara peels the paper from one cake.

KARA (CONT'D)  
On Earth, you stick candles on the cake. One for every year. You make a wish when you blow them out.

RUTHYE  
You light the cake on fire?

KARA  
No. You stick candles on it.

Ruthye makes a face.

KARA (CONT'D)  
I thought it was weird too, but it's... sort of nice.

Ruthye studies the bare frosting.

RUTHYE  
There are no candles.

KARA  
It's a food court.

Kara takes one bite. Her wrist tag pulses with the clinic's amber status light. She stops chewing.

Ruthye moves the less damaged cupcake to Kara's side of the tray.

RUTHYE  
Eat.

Kara looks at her.

RUTHYE (CONT'D)  
He is still breathing.

Kara picks up the broth.

They eat because there is work left to do.

### 16. INT. OASIS STATION — WASHROOM — LATER

The concourse falls to a muffled hum behind a closing door.

Kara is there before Krypto's weight settles onto the table. She puts her forehead against his.

KARA  
Hi.

Krypto opens his eyes.

His tail hits the table once.

Again.

Then the whole dog tries to stand at once.

Kara laughs into his fur and holds on while he covers her face in exhausted kisses.

Ruthye puts a hand against his side and feels the strong heart beneath it.

The Robodoc watches the monitor return to green.

ROBODOC  
Best dog in the universe. Confirmed.

Beyond the clinic glass, work continues in the repair bay.

Vesk closes a service grille on Kara's ship. Inside the cleaner Kryptonian drive assembly, the amber Brigand jump-seed turns in its dark casing, held by mismatched brackets and fresh leads.

Vesk disconnects the last umbilical.

### 33. EXT. OASIS STATION / DEEP SPACE — LATER

Kara's ship clears the dock under its own power.

The foreign amber light pulses once through the open drive vents.

The ship jumps.

### 34. EXT. EVELIE — KNOLL HOMESTEAD — RED EVENING

The repaired kite flies above the same pale fields.

Tomme runs beneath it. Marye works near the porch.

A ship crosses the red sun.

Marye drops what she is holding.

The ship settles beside the road. Its ramp lowers.

Ruthye appears at the top.

Marye runs.

Ruthye makes it three steps down the ramp before her mother reaches her. Marye gathers her in with enough force to lift her from the ground.

For one breath Ruthye tries to remain upright inside it.

Then she holds her mother like a child.

RUTHYE  
I promised I would.

MARYE  
I know.

Tomme collides with Ruthye's waist. She drops to her knees and pulls him in too.

He touches the healed cut at her brow.

TOMME  
Did you kill him?

Ruthye looks over his head at Kara.

Kara stands at the ramp with Krypto beside her.

RUTHYE  
The debt has been repaid.

Tomme frowns, trying to solve that.

Marye looks from her daughter to Kara and understands.

She closes her eyes and holds both children closer.

Krypto chooses that moment to charge into the reunion.

Tomme disappears beneath white fur and delighted barking. Marye laughs. Ruthye holds on.

Kara stays beside the ship, watching the family become ordinary again.

Marye crosses to her.

MARYE  
You brought her home.

KARA  
She saved my life.

Marye takes Kara's hands, then pulls her into the embrace before Kara can decide what to do.

Kara lets herself be held.

LATER —

Warm light fills the farmhouse windows. Tomme runs past with Krypto and the kite tail streaming behind them.

Kara and Ruthye stand near the ship.

KARA  
You know, I never did get a proper birthday cake.

Ruthye remembers the two sad cupcakes. The candles that were not there.

RUTHYE  
What will you wish for?

Krypto races up the ramp.

Kara looks at him. At Ruthye. At Marye and Tomme in the warm doorway.

KARA  
I already got it.

Ruthye laughs.

Not the almost-laugh from the road. A bright, surprised sound she cannot call back.

She turns toward the house.

RUTHYE  
Mama! We have to get Kara a birthday cake. We'll be back tonight!

MARYE  
A what?

Ruthye runs up the ramp after Krypto.

KARA  
Tomorrow at the latest.

Marye gives Kara a look.

KARA (CONT'D)  
Tonight.

Kara follows Ruthye aboard.

The ship rises through the red evening. Ruthye is still laughing in the cockpit window.

Marye and Tomme wave until it becomes a light in the sky.
